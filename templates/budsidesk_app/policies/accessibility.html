Our Commitment

At BudsiDesk, we believe everyone should be able to access and use our platform easily, regardless of ability or technology. We are committed to making our website and app compliant with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1.

Measures We Take

We aim to ensure accessibility by:

  • Using clear, consistent layouts and navigation.
  • Providing alternative text for all images.
  • Supporting keyboard navigation and screen readers.
  • Testing our interface with accessibility tools.

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is a continuous process. We regularly review our features, collect feedback, and make improvements to enhance usability for all users.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full compliance, some third-party content (e.g., embedded widgets or analytics dashboards) may not yet meet all accessibility standards. We are working with partners to improve these areas.

Feedback

If you experience any accessibility issues or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:
admin@budsidesk.com

We aim to respond within 5 business days.
