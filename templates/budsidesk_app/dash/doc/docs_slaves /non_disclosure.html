2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by the Disclosing Party, including but not limited to:

  • Business plans and commercial strategies
  • Data, trade secrets and know-how
  • Technical materials and software
  • Customer and supplier information
  • Any information marked as confidential

3. Receiving Party Obligations

The Receiving Party agrees to:

  • Maintain all Confidential Information in strict confidence
  • Not disclose it to third parties without written consent
  • Use the Confidential Information only for the agreed purposes
  • Implement reasonable security measures to protect the information

4. Exceptions

The above obligations do not apply to information that:

  • Was already known without violation of any NDA
  • Becomes public without fault of the Receiving Party
  • Is disclosed with written consent
  • Must be disclosed by law (with prior notification)

Legal Terms

5. Return or Destruction

Upon request of the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information.

6. No License

Nothing in this Agreement grants rights over the Confidential Information except as expressly stated.
