FAQs – BudsiDesk Plans

1. What's included in the free Budsi Lite plan?

Budsi Lite gives you the essentials to get your solo business organised, invoicing, receipts tracking, taxation insights and a personal dashboard. It's totally free, with no hidden fees.

2. Can I try a paid plan before committing?

Yes! You can start with Budsi Smart with a 7-day free trial. Cancel anytime during the trial if it's not for you.

3. What's the difference between Budsi Smart and Budsi Elite?

Budsi Smart gives you structure, and Budsi Elite gives you power. Choose Smart to stay organised or Elite to automate, analyse, and grow.

4. Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

Nope! All plans are billed monthly or annually; you can cancel or switch anytime. Annual plans come with a nice discount, too!

5. Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

Absolutely. You can change your plan at the end of your current monthly contract.

6. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide range of payment methods powered by Stripe, including:

  • Credit & Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Apple Pay & Google Pay
  • Revolut Pay
7. What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You'll keep full access to your paid plan until the end of your billing period. After that, your account will switch to Budsi Lite, no data will be lost.

8. Is BudsiDesk available in my country?

BudsiDesk is built for solo traders and freelancers in Ireland. More regions coming soon! NEW

9. Is my financial data safe with BudsiDesk?

Your trust is our priority. We design our systems to keep your data safe, compliant, and confidential. Importantly, we never access your bank account — we only process payments related to your subscription. We also fully comply with GDPR, ensuring your information remains secure, private, and handled with care.

FAQs – BudsiDesk Modules

Invoices and Credit Notes

10. Can I create customised invoices?

Yes! You can create and personalise invoices with your logo or/and adjust the template. Your details and clients' info are saved in your favourites for next time.

11. Can I send invoices directly from BudsiDesk?

Absolutely. You can send them via email or download a PDF version to share manually.

12. Can I add my VAT number and bank account?

Yes. Just make sure to enter the correct details:

  • IBAN: 22 characters, starting with "IE" (no spaces)
  • VAT Reg: 8–9 alphanumeric characters

Tax

13. Does BudsiDesk calculate my taxes automatically?

Yes! The Tax Report gives you real-time tax estimates as you input data.

14. Can I download a summary for my tax return?

Yes, in the Tax Report section you can generate and download reports for return reference.

15. Will BudsiDesk remind me of tax deadlines?

Definitely. You'll get friendly reminders about VAT returns, Form 11 deadlines, and other compliance dates.

Track

16. Can I log my working hours?

Yes. The Track module lets you record your hours daily or weekly for each project recorded.

17. Can I track progress by client or project?

Absolutely! BudsiDesk gives you visual productivity insights by client, project, or period.

BudsiWhiz and Helpdesk

18. Where can I learn more about using the platform?

In BudsiWhiz, you'll find handy walkthroughs, quick actions, and helpful resources tailored for freelancers.

19. Can I reach real human support?

Yes — through the Helpdesk. Our team offers real, human help (no bots!).

Buzz

20. What reminders can I set up?

You can enable reminders in Budsi Buzz section adding them in the calendar for:

  • Invoice payments
  • Tax deadlines
  • Subscription renewals
  • Projects deadlines
21. How will I receive reminders?

You choose — via email or dashboard alerts.
